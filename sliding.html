Why a Sliding Scale?

The Community Acupuncture Clinic of Arcata provides acupuncture treatments and herbal medicine consults for 20-40 dollars (cash only) a session. You decide how much you want to pay each time you come in. This sliding scale helps make acupuncture more accessible to our community, and it offers a degree of flexibility to pay a bit more or a bit less for any reason. For instance, some people might want to pay a little less when they need to come in more frequently and may feel comfortable paying a little more when they come in less often, whereas others might want to pay the same amount every time they come in. Again, this is completely up to you.
